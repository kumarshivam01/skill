HTML5 Features Showcase

Feature 1: Article Tag

The <article> tag is used to represent a self-contained piece of content that could be distributed and reused independently. It is suitable for articles, blog posts, comments, or any other independent content block.

Feature 2: Video Tag

The <video> tag allows you to embed video content directly into your HTML. It supports various video formats and provides a native way to control playback, volume, and more.

Feature 3: Canvas Tag

The <canvas> tag provides a drawable region on your web page using JavaScript. It is commonly used for creating dynamic graphics, animations, and interactive content.
